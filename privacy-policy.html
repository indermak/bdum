Switch Transaction

  • This switch facility allows you the transfer your investment from an existing scheme to a new scheme.
  • This new scheme may be of the same Mutual Fund company or some other Mutual Fund company.
  • This transaction is a redemption transaction from SWITCH-OUT scheme and purchase transaction in SWITCH-IN scheme.
  • Based on your instruction, a Redemption transaction from the SWITCH-OUT scheme and a purchase transaction in the SWITCH -IN scheme would be carried out.
  • Your redemption proceeds shall be subject to applicable exit load (if any) and applicable taxes.
  • The purchase transaction in the SWITCH -IN scheme will be initiated on the day the redemption proceeds are received from the executed redemption transaction from SWITCH-OUT scheme.
  • If because of any reason your redemption transaction from the SWITCH-OUT scheme is rejected by registrar, your purchase transaction in the SWITCH -IN scheme will not be executed.
  • As mutual fund transactions are done on prospective NAV basis only, you confirm / agree that Bajaj Capital Limited or any of its group company will not be held responsible for any change in the market value / NAV on the day of purchase transaction.
  • Every mutual fund scheme has minimum investment amount along with the multiples in which amount can be invested. While executing your SWITCH-IN transaction, Bajaj Capital Limited will consider the amount which is nearest to the actual redemption proceeds and is in multiple of the amount specified by fund house for the SWITCH-IN scheme. This may result in some remainder between the actual redemption proceeds from SWITCH-OUT scheme and amount invested in SWITCH-IN scheme. This remainder (i.e. differential amount) will be credited to your bank account.
  • If actual redemption proceeds from SWITCH-OUT transaction is less than minimum investment amount allowed in Switch-IN transaction, the SWITCH-IN transaction will not be executed. The actual redemption proceeds will be credited to your bank account. You confirm / agree that Bajaj Capital Limited or any of its group company will not be held responsible for any possible reinvestment loss / exit load applicability / change in the market value / NAV on the day of purchase transaction.
  • If you already have a folio number in the Switch-IN AMCs (which is displayed under holdings tab), your purchase transaction will be initiated in the same folio number. In all other cases, new folio will be created.
