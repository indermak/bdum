About OneBajaj.Capital

Bajaj Capital Ltd. is one of the leading and trusted wealth management companies in India. We offer need based financial solutions to individual investors, corporate houses, institutional investors, Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and High Net worth clients, among others. We are focused on delivering an omni-channel experience backed through a technology enabled touch model to our clients.

Who we are

BFor the last 53 years, Bajaj Capital has been helping the third generation of investors, creating and protecting wealth with close to 3.9 million clients. Today, we serve clients through a combination of technology & touch model i.e. Bajaj Capital One - a one stop wealth management platform enabling a team of 4,500 colleagues at 270 points-of-presence (POP) in more than 100 cities.

How we empower you

Bajaj Capital provides a wide range of wealth creation products ranging from insurance, mutual funds, corporate fixed deposits, real estate, loans among others through qualified and experienced professionals including CAs, MBAs, MBEs, CFPs, CSs, Legal Experts, and around 2500 Wealth Managers serving clients from mass retail to high net worth individuals. Our company is profitably growing with growth in business volumes and revenues supported by improved productivity. Since 2017, the net worth of the company has almost doubled.

Who we are

We have always been front-runners in our area of operations.
